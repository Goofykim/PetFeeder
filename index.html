Aim and Objectives

  • To provide efficiency and more convenient way for pet owners who are not able to feed their pets as they are away from home for days or several reasons.
  • Introduce the concept of a smart pet feeding machine using Arduino
  • We also want to try to observe the solutions for modern days' problems and take them as the tests for our creativity.
